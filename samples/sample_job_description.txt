Senior Backend Engineer

Company: CloudScale Technologies
Location: Remote (US-based)
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role
We are seeking a Senior Backend Engineer to join our growing platform team. You will be responsible for designing, building, and scaling the distributed systems that power our real-time analytics platform. This role requires deep expertise in Python, cloud infrastructure, and microservices architecture.

What You'll Do
- Design and develop high-performance backend services using Python, FastAPI, and Django.
- Build and maintain scalable microservices deployed on AWS using Docker and Kubernetes.
- Optimize database performance with PostgreSQL and Redis, ensuring low-latency data access.
- Architect and manage CI/CD pipelines using GitHub Actions and Terraform.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams in an Agile environment to deliver high-quality software.
- Mentor junior engineers and lead technical design discussions.
- Build data pipelines with Apache Spark and Apache Airflow for real-time analytics.

Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of professional experience in backend software engineering.
- Strong proficiency in Python and at least one other language (Go, Java, or Rust preferred).
- Experience building REST APIs and microservices at scale.
- Hands-on experience with AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes.
- Deep knowledge of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis).
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform).
- Strong understanding of distributed systems and system design.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with Apache Kafka and event-driven architectures.
- Familiarity with machine learning model deployment and MLOps.
- Knowledge of GraphQL and gRPC.
- Experience with monitoring tools like Prometheus and Grafana.
- AWS or Kubernetes certification.
- Experience with test-driven development and automated testing.

Nice to Have
- Experience with React or Vue.js for full-stack contributions.
- Knowledge of Snowflake or BigQuery for data warehousing.
- Contributions to open-source projects.

Benefits
- Competitive salary ($160,000 — $200,000)
- Remote-first culture
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match
- Professional development budget
